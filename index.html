Why Plastic Is Harmful

Plastic pollutes land, water and air. It harms animals, marine life and human health through microplastics.

How to Reduce Plastic

  • Avoid single-use plastic
  • Use cloth or jute bags
  • Recycle plastic properly
  • Spread awareness

Tree Plantation & Cooperation

Planting trees helps fight climate change, improves air quality and protects biodiversity.
